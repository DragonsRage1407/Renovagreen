About

Welcome to Nova Points

A revolutionary project from a heart for the world, building sustainability, and giving communities the power to empower themselves. Renovagreen, a company incorporated in 2024 in Apex, North Carolina, was born to address a persistent problem regarding improper recycling while simultaneously bolstering local businesses. At Nova Points, we believe that every action counts, and the small steps we take today can have a significant impact on the preservation of our earth and the lives of future generations. The problem of improper recycling had been observed with extreme concern in Apex, North Carolina, with its trend emerging as a problem dragging the environment into a state of degradation. Our dedicated team of people brought together with a shared vision of incentivizing and rewarding responsible behavior toward recycling. Through innovative solutions and strategic partnerships, Nova Points has within its dreams to become the spearhead of positive change in our community.

We take two steps toward achievement: We offer exciting rewards to those who recycle correctly and provide incentives to local businesses to opt for eco-friendly strategies. By motivating the public to recycle right, we reduce waste and cultivate a culture of environmental stewardship. By way of partnership program, we empower businesses to implement sustainable strategies that generate economic growth while keeping their carbon footprint low. We believe in transparency, integrity, and a measure of impact. We think collaboration—via input from stakeholders—is the key to our growing programs and services. Together, we can work out a way to a more sustainable future for Apex, North Carolina, and the world. Join Nova Points in our journey toward a greener and healthier planet, one recyclable item and one local business at a time.
