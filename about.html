About

Welcome to EcoRewards, a pioneering company born out of a passion for sustainability and community empowerment. Established in 2024 in Apex, North Carolina, EcoRewards was founded with a clear mission: to tackle the pressing issue of improper recycling while simultaneously supporting local businesses. At EcoRewards, we believe that every action, no matter how small, can make a significant difference in preserving our planet for future generations.

In Apex, North Carolina, we recognized a concerning trend of improper recycling practices leading to environmental degradation. Our team of dedicated individuals came together with a shared vision to incentivize and reward responsible recycling behaviors. Through innovative solutions and strategic partnerships, EcoRewards has become a catalyst for positive change in our community.

Our approach is twofold: we offer enticing rewards to individuals who recycle properly and provide incentives for local businesses to adopt eco-friendly practices. By encouraging residents to recycle correctly, we not only reduce waste but also promote a culture of environmental stewardship. Through our partnership program, we empower businesses to implement sustainable initiatives, driving economic growth while minimizing their carbon footprint.

At EcoRewards, we are committed to transparency, integrity, and measurable impact. We believe in the power of collaboration and actively engage with stakeholders to continuously improve our programs and services. Together, we strive to create a more sustainable future for Apex, North Carolina, and beyond.
